Krugmantimes.com is a webpage that takes the current version of the New York Times and replaces pretty much everything with Paul Krugman media. This means swapping out the masthead, parsing articles for keywords and putting in econometrics gibberish, "fixing" the bylines, and replacing most of the images with Paul's sweet, sweet mug.

This is a Node.js app, and the source code is freely available. It uses Redis as a caching layer on top of the NYT as well as the keyword extraction service provided by Alchemy. If you'd like to talk about anything at all, you're more than welcome to email me at me@vincentwoo.com.

Vincent Woo, March 2013
